## 💰 Unit Economics (The Real Math)

### Per Customer Economics
```
Average Revenue Per Customer (ARPC):     $165/month
Customer Lifetime (avg):                 36 months (3 years)
Gross Margin:                            85% (SaaS typical)

Lifetime Value (LTV):
  $165/month × 36 months × 85% margin = $5,049

Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):         $400 (blended)

LTV:CAC Ratio:                           12.6:1 ✅ (Excellent! >3:1 is good)

Payback Period:
  $400 CAC ÷ ($165 × 85% margin) = 2.9 months ✅ (Great! <12 months is good)

Monthly Profit Per Customer:
  ($165 × 85%) - ($451 infra ÷ 100 customers) = $136/month
```

### Break-Even Analysis
```
Fixed Costs:
  Infrastructure:            $451/month
  Marketing:                 $5,000/month
  Your Salary (minimal):     $3,000/month
  Tools & Software:          $500/month
  Legal & Accounting:        $500/month
  -----------------------------------
  TOTAL FIXED:               $9,451/month

Variable Costs:
  Transaction fees:          3% of revenue
  Support (outsourced):      $10 per customer/month

Break-Even Calculation:
  Revenue needed:            $9,451 ÷ 82% margin = $11,526/month
  Customers needed:          $11,526 ÷ $165 = 70 customers

TIME TO BREAK-EVEN:          Month 9 (based on projections)
```
