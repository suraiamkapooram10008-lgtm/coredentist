# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 12, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 12, 2026

## Introduction

CoreDent PMS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our dental practice management system.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, role
- **Patient Health Information (PHI):** Medical history, dental records, treatment plans, clinical notes
- **Billing Information:** Payment methods, invoices, transaction history
- **Practice Information:** Practice name, address, staff details

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Performance Data:** Load times, errors, crashes

### Cookies and Tracking
- **Essential Cookies:** Authentication, session management, CSRF protection
- **Analytics Cookies:** Usage patterns, feature adoption (with consent)
- **Preference Cookies:** Language, theme, display settings

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Service Delivery:** Provide and maintain the dental practice management system
- **Patient Care:** Enable healthcare providers to deliver quality dental care
- **Billing:** Process payments and generate invoices
- **Communication:** Send appointment reminders, notifications, updates
- **Support:** Respond to inquiries and provide customer support

### Secondary Uses
- **Analytics:** Improve features, understand usage patterns
- **Security:** Detect fraud, prevent abuse, ensure data security
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and other regulations

## HIPAA Compliance

CoreDent PMS is designed to be HIPAA-compliant:

- **Business Associate Agreement (BAA):** Available upon request
- **PHI Protection:** All patient health information is encrypted and secured
- **Access Controls:** Role-based permissions limit data access
- **Audit Logs:** All PHI access is logged and monitored
- **Breach Notification:** We will notify you of any data breaches within 60 days

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information or PHI to third parties.

### Limited Sharing
We may share information with:

- **Service Providers:** Hosting (AWS/DigitalOcean), email (SendGrid), monitoring (Sentry)
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe/Square for payment processing
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect rights and safety

### Your Control
- You control who in your practice can access patient data
- Patients can request copies of their data
- You can export all your data at any time

## Data Security

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Authentication:** JWT tokens with short expiration, refresh token rotation
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions, multi-factor authentication ready
- **CSRF Protection:** Token-based protection on all state-changing operations
- **Rate Limiting:** Protection against brute force attacks

### Administrative Safeguards
- **Security Training:** Regular staff security awareness training
- **Incident Response:** Documented breach notification procedures
- **Access Reviews:** Regular review of user access and permissions
- **Vendor Management:** All vendors sign BAAs and undergo security review

### Physical Safeguards
- **Data Centers:** SOC 2 Type II certified facilities
- **Redundancy:** Multi-region backups and disaster recovery
- **Access Logs:** All physical and logical access is logged

## Your Rights

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Portability:** Export your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain data processing
- **Right to Restrict:** Limit how we process your data

### CCPA Rights (California Users)
- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### HIPAA Rights (All Users)
- **Right to Access:** Access your PHI within 30 days
- **Right to Amend:** Request corrections to PHI
- **Right to Accounting:** List of PHI disclosures
- **Right to Restrict:** Request restrictions on PHI use
- **Right to Confidential Communications:** Choose how we contact you

### How to Exercise Your Rights
Email: privacy@coredent.com  
Response Time: Within 30 days  
Verification: We may request identity verification

## Data Retention

### Active Data
- **Patient Records:** Retained as long as practice maintains relationship
- **Billing Records:** 7 years (IRS requirement)
- **Audit Logs:** 6 years (HIPAA requirement)

### Deleted Data
- **Soft Delete:** Data marked inactive but retained for recovery
- **Hard Delete:** Permanent deletion after 90 days (upon request)
- **Backup Retention:** Backups retained for 30 days

### Account Deletion
When you delete your account:
1. All data is marked for deletion
2. PHI is anonymized or deleted per HIPAA requirements
3. Billing records retained per legal requirements
4. Complete deletion within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

CoreDent PMS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us immediately.
